We are on a war footing to ensure our clinic is COVID Green. We have multiple layers of precautions that we have to keep our patients and our staffs safe from this virus.

How we keep our Clinic safe during COVID-19 pandemic
Featured
We have a dedicated COVID screening desk through which every person be it patient,attender staffs and also our heroes “the doctors& nurses” are screened for temperature, hand sanitized and provided masks.
Featured
All patients and attenders are required to fill in a self-declaration form, that asks you questions such as Travel History, contact with any suspected or positive COVID patients, etc.
Featured
Every individual in the hospital premises is mandated to wear masks.
Featured
Sanitizers are placed strategically throughout the hospital to ensure that frequent hand sanitization is maintained by everyone.
Featured
Any patient before the start of the treatment is advised to undergo a COVID-19 test, this ensures that all our patients are safe.
Featured
During the period when the COVID-19 results are awaited the treatment will not be stopped and the treatment will continue with all necessary precautions.
